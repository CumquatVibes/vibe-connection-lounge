Welcome to Vibe Bazaar

We're stoked to have you in the Vibe Connection Lounge marketplace.

This is where your brand meets our community. Here's the lowdown on how we grow together:

The Deal

We keep it simple with a flat 10% commission on every sale. No hidden fees, just growth. Payouts are processed monthly directly to your account.

Where Your 10% Goes

Every commission directly funds the growth of our community:

  • ✓ Platform improvements to enhance your selling experience
  • ✓ Bringing in expert speakers & mentors for exclusive 1-on-1 sessions
  • ✓ Free mentorship access for premium website subscribers
  • ✓ Community events that drive traffic to vendor products

Your Lane

You're in full control of your products. Vendors handle their own:

  • ✓ Shipping: You choose your carriers and rates
  • ✓ Packaging: Brand it your way
  • ✓ Tracking updates: Keep customers in the loop

This ensures your customers get the vibe you intended, exactly how you want it delivered.

The Community

Your brand gets featured across the Lounge, including our "Vibe Bazaar" highlights. You'll be part of a curated marketplace focused on wellness, lifestyle, and digital tools for entrepreneurs who are building momentum.
